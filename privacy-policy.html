ClipStack Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

ClipStack is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, transmit, store, or sell any personal or sensitive data. All clipboard history is stored locally on your device and never leaves your browser.

Data We Access

ClipStack only processes text that you copy using your system clipboard.

We do not monitor or track:

  • Page content
  • Keystrokes
  • Form data
  • Login credentials
  • Browsing history
  • User identity

Where Your Data Is Stored

All clipboard entries are stored securely in your browser using Chrome’s storage.local system.

  • No cloud servers
  • No third-party databases
  • No external accounts required

You may delete your clipboard history at any time. Uninstalling the extension instantly clears all data.

Permissions Justification

Permission Why It’s Needed What It Does NOT Do
storage Saves your clipboard history locally. Does not sync, upload, or share data.
clipboardWrite Allows ClipStack to paste items when you choose them. Does not read the clipboard automatically.
scripting Injects the clipboard capture script only when needed. Does not alter or track web pages.
tabs Allows the popup to know which tab is active for inserting text. Does not read browsing history or URLs.
content_scripts (<all_urls>) Detects copy/cut actions on pages. Does not access or analyze page content.

Data Collection & Sharing

ClipStack does not:

  • Collect or store personal data
  • Send data to external servers
  • Share data with third parties
  • Use analytics or tracking

Your clipboard history stays on your device, under your control.

User Control

You may:

  • Clear clipboard history anytime
  • Disable monitoring
  • Uninstall to remove all stored data
